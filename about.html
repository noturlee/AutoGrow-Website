AutoGrow's Story

AutoGrow is a hydroponics team that came together as part of their Work Integrated Learning, which is a module for the institution known as Varsity College. The team consists of six members: Leighche Jaikarran, Naiya Haribhai, Lubnah Olideen, Vikhayle Sewnundan, Tanya Govender, and Talhah Motala. Leighche Jaikarran and Naiya Haribhai are the co-leaders and software architects.

AutoGrow is working on a project called SmartHydro, which is an AI-powered hydroponics system enclosed in a tent. The AI is known as Fred.
